INTERNATIONAL AND LOCAL COLLABORATION AND LINKAGES OFFICE

    Functions of the International and Local Collaboration and Linkages Office:

  • Act as the clearing house for local and international MOUs and MOAs for endorsement to the Administrative Council and Board of Regents;
  • Identify and discuss issues to the Executive Council that may impact on University’s Internationalization Strategy;
  • Respond to proposals referred by Colleges/Campuses that relate to local and international linkages;
  • Ensure transfer of information, knowledge and identification of linkages in both local and international to the Academic Council of Colleges and Campuses;
  • Provide forum for the development and exchange of good practices in international and local activities;
  • Serve as liaison officer in relation to local and international research and academic collaboration with other agencies; and
  • Manage all communication/ documents in relation to student and faculty exchanges, study visit and the like between and among academic partners.

GENDER AND DEVELOPMENT RESOURCE CENTER

Vision Mission
A gender-responsive educational institution where stakeholders enjoy equal responsibilities and opportunities. CvSU–GAD Resource Center shall integrate and advocate gender equity and equality principles and perspectives in providing instruction, research, and extension services.
Goals:
The GAD Resource Center shall aim for:

1.Sustainable and gender responsive instruction, research, community development and involvement;
2.Gender equity and equality among the academic community;
3.Sustainable partnership and cooperation among stakeholders; and
4.Gender responsive governance.
